St. Mathews Sesinia School was founded in February 2007 by Peter and Esther. Run by our local Cowalenga CBO, and affiliated with Plan International. To identify and assist all orphaned and vulnerable children out of school due to lack of school fees access higher education through establishment of institutions of higher learning and enable them realize their full potential. Two orphans, Josephine and Fredrick Odhiambo, were our first students.
